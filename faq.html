FAQ

Frequently asked questions

We'll do our best to answer all your questions. For anything else, call or message us at 702.490.9449 or 725.213.0049.

What is Elite Medical Suites?

Elite Medical Suites provides a comprehensive clinic setup to practitioners of various disciplines, removing the operational and financial challenges of starting a clinic. More than just a workplace, it's a community of healthcare professionals — with modern technology, flexible workspaces, and an atmosphere that encourages collaboration and growth.

Are the workspaces furnished?

Yes — workspaces are fully furnished with exam and consultation rooms, basic medical supplies, and modern office furniture so providers can focus solely on their patients.

What member benefits are included?

Members have 24/7 facility access, shared spaces, a provider lounge, breakroom, mail and packages, plus a curated list of exclusive membership benefits supporting business, productivity, and marketing.

Is on-site parking available?

There are approximately 100 parking spots in the Corporate Center. Assigned Elite Med Suites spots are marked; we kindly ask that patients be given priority.

What amenities are included in the membership fee?

No extra fees for common area maintenance, real estate taxes, utilities, janitorial services, WiFi, use of facility furniture and equipment, and basic medical supplies stocked in the rooms. Sharps and medical waste disposal are also provided.

Are there extra charges for printing, mail, or Wi-Fi?

No. Elite Med Suites offers an all-inclusive membership package with one monthly fee.

Does front desk staff answer incoming calls?

We don't provide call answering, since the facility is shared by multiple practices. We recommend a virtual assistant or answering service — EMS is an excellent resource for virtual assistant services.

Is there a minimum booking requirement?

No. You're never required to book. Several members use our office only for business purposes like mail and deliveries. Bookings have a one-hour minimum, then 30-minute increments.

Do you provide administrative or medical assistance services?

Yes. Members can book Medical Assistant (MA) services as needed, plus Virtual Assistant services for business setup, admin support, EHR setup, billing, and more. Contact our team for a quote.

How do I sign up to be a member?

Book a self-guided tour through Calendly or sign up directly through our membership portal to get started.

Do you charge a percentage of billings or profits?

No. You keep 100% of your billings. We charge for clinic space and services — never a share of your revenue.

Is there a sign-up fee or security deposit?

A refundable security deposit is required: $300 for basic monthly memberships, $1,000 for full-time/exclusive room plans. Both are fully refundable at the end of your stay, subject to timely payments and no excessive damage.

What is the commitment term?

Hourly or part-time plans end anytime with one month's notice — never locked in beyond a month. Full-time exclusive rooms carry a 12-month initial term plus a 3-month notice period.

Can I store supplies and belongings on-site?

Yes. Lockers are provided for small equipment and supplies, lockable refrigerated storage for medications, and larger equipment may be stored subject to approval and availability.

Can I promote my business in the waiting area?

Yes. EMS displays a full-screen practice profile on the waiting room TV, members can place cards and brochures in designated areas, member profiles are added to our website, and networking events are held periodically.

Will I get the same exam room every booking?

On hourly plans, you choose from any available room at booking — the same room isn't guaranteed. Only full-time exclusive room plans guarantee the same room.

Can I use more than one room at the same time?

Yes, subject to availability.

Is this a lease?

No. Elite Medical Suites is not a traditional lease — it's a flexible, membership-based medical coworking space where providers access fully equipped clinic rooms as needed.

What happens if I'm late on payments?

Late fees apply after 5 days past due. Facility access may be suspended if payment isn't received within 10 days.

Can I decorate my room?

Only full-time Private Suite members may personalize rooms, with EMS approval. Permanent changes aren't allowed.

How does reception and patient flow work?

EMS uses a self-check-in model with a virtual receptionist and kiosk. Patients check in digitally on arrival and providers are notified — maintaining privacy, efficiency, and a low-traffic clinical environment.

How far in advance do I need to book?

No strict minimum — same-day bookings are possible based on availability. Book ahead for preferred times. A 6-hour cancellation window applies.

How does EMS reduce my overhead costs?

Members avoid long-term leases and high fixed expenses — saving on rent, staffing, utilities, and equipment by paying only for the space and time used. Often reducing overhead by 60% or more versus traditional leases.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to walk you through plans, the space, and how to get started.
