Plan Details & Policies

How the Virtual Membership works

This tier is administrative and branding support only. By enrolling, the member acknowledges that no on-site access is permitted except under the limited exceptions below.

Use of the EMS address

Permitted for licensing, insurance, credentialing, professional marketing, and administrative correspondence. Non-exclusive, non-transferable, and limited strictly to the member's professional activities. The member is responsible for verifying that the EMS address satisfies any licensing board, payer, regulator, or agency requirements; EMS does not guarantee compliance suitability.

Mail & package handling

EMS receives and sorts your business mail and packages with a text notification on arrival. Items are held for pickup. Forwarding is available on request for $25 plus shipping cost per shipment. Excessive volume, restricted items, or misuse may result in suspension of mail privileges.

No walk-ins or onsite activity

No receiving patients or clients onsite and no conducting business operations within the facility. No bringing guests, visitors, or inspectors onsite. No use of EMS rooms, coworking areas, or common spaces. Members may not present themselves as having physical access to EMS facilities.

Onsite access — mail & package pickup only

The only onsite access available to Virtual Members is for mail and package pickup, for a $50 access fee, during office hours only, scheduled and coordinated in advance with the EMS support team.

Regulatory audit provisions

EMS may accurately disclose to regulators that the member holds a Virtual Membership with no physical-access privileges, and may decline onsite inspection requests not included in the plan. If physical access is required for compliance, EMS may require an upgrade to a physical-access plan.

Membership terms

$99/month billed month to month · no long-term commitment · $300 refundable security deposit · $100 one-time setup fee · cancel anytime with 30 days' notice · valid for one EMS location · out-of-state licensure permitted · no facility or room access (upgrade to an in-person membership for access).
